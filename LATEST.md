GameModifiers v1.0.0

Adds a checkbox panel of stackable game modifiers to the difficulty / mode select screen.

Helpful: Brittle Bloons, Sharp Shots, Piercing Power, Eagle Eye, Rapid Fire
Harmful: Iron Bloons, Need for Speed, Swarm, Sluggish Towers, Tunnel Vision

Tick any combination before starting a game. Nothing is on by default; everything stacks.
